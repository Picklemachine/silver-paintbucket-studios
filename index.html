The Legend of Bucky

Curator. Creator. Connoisseur. Mascot.

Silver Paintbucket Studios was born from a simple idea: that art shouldn't be locked away in stiff, silent museums. It should be messy, alive, and filled with character. That's where Bucky comes in.

Our stylish, mustache-wearing, silver-plated guide has traveled the globe searching for independent painters who bring raw canvas to life. With a paintbrush in his hand and a wooden palette in his paw, Bucky represents the playful, unpretentious spirit of our creative community.
